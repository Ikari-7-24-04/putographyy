PRODUCT CONCEPT

The P.U.T.O in P.U.T.O.graphy stands for P – Preserve, U – Unforgettable memories, T – Together in every, O – Occasion. The idea of the product or application/service is to preserve important events in our lives such as ceremonies, birthdays, graduation, marriages, anniversaries, or even simple events.

P.U.T.O.graphy is perfect for people who are planning to take photos for a special occasion in their lives or just wants to have fun and have something to remember that moment for.
